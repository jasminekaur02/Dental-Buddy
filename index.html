FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is AI Dental Analysis?
AI Dental Analysis uses artificial intelligence to analyze dental radiographs and CBCT scans, aiding in the identification of various dental conditions.
How do I upload images for analysis?
Navigate to the "Get CBCT Scan" section, click the button, and follow the instructions to upload your dental images for real-time analysis.
Is the analysis secure?
Yes, the system prioritizes the security and privacy of patient data. Images are processed securely, and access is restricted to authorized users.
How can I provide feedback or report issues?
We appreciate your feedback. You can use the provided feedback form on the website or contact our support team at support@aidentalanalysis.com.
What types of dental conditions can the system detect?
The system can detect a wide range of dental conditions, including cavities, bone loss, impacted teeth, TMJ disorders, and other anomalies commonly observed in dental radiographs and CBCT scans.
